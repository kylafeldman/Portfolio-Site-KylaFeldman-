About Me

Who am I?

I’m a UI/UX designer with a natural tendency to empathize with people and create user-centric design solutions. I’ve worked on a variety of digital design projects as well as freelance web designs, which is what ultimately led me to UI design. I’m passionate about designing for users, and my background in graphic design helps me create UI designs that are data-driven, aesthetically pleasing, and pixel perfect. I welcome collaboration and can implement new ideas and processes quickly and effectively, helping to streamline workflows and communication. I am eager to find a career in UI/UX design that furthers my passion for sustainability, accessibility, and commitment to social good.

When I'm not designing, you'll likely find me in a park with friends, exploring Oregon, doodling, cooking, going to local concerts, and reading!
